📋 Rules
General
  • 4-man teams, Ryder Cup style
  • All OB played as lateral — 2 club length drop from the point of entry, no closer to the hole
  • Stonington CC local rule — no relief from stone walls, play the ball as it lies
  • Ball in a divot (fairway only) — nearest point of relief, no closer to the hole
Format Rules
⛳ Day 1 — 2 Man Scramble
HCP35% low man · 15% high man PTS0.5 per stroke better (max 3)
  • Stroke play, team vs. team
  • 6 drives minimum per player
  • Place in fairway & bunkers, drop in the rough
  • 1 club length drop, no closer to the hole
  • Putts — 1 putter head length, no closer to the hole
⛳ Day 2 — Bodega
HCP80% PTS3 per match
Low match+2
High match+1
Long drive (P5)+1
Closest pin (P3)+1
Gross birdie+1
Gross eagle+2
3 putts−0.5
Long drive: must be in fairway · Closest pin: must be on green
⛳ Day 3 — John Daly (2v2 Scramble)
HCP35% low man · 15% high man PTS0.5 per stroke better (max 3)
  • Stroke play, team vs. team
  • 24 beers per team — each beer consumed = −1 shot from team score
  • Unlimited mulligans — 1 tequila shot each
  • Place in fairway & bunkers, drop in the rough
  • 1 club length drop, no closer to the hole
  • Putts — 1 putter head length, no closer to the hole
⛳ Day 4 — Singles Matches
HCP100% — mulligans, not strokes PTS3 per match
  • 1v1 match play

    e.g. 4 strokes = flat match + 4 mulligans
